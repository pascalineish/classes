Benefits of doing practice

Practicing yoga or going to gym is very difficult to choose.They are little bit related.
Doing yoga before to the gym workout reduce the risk of injury and help to warm up the body. Yoga after gym workout helps to relax the muscles. In gym we generally burn more calories than we take.
This will result in weight loss and muscle buildup. But yoga works very slowly in weight loss.
According to Mayo Clinic, a person burns around 240 calories in 1 hour yoga; the same person burns 360 calories during an hour of exercise. Yoga helps in weight controlling.
Yoga and gym have their impact to make our body healthy and fit.
